Medal Of Honor

This website is going to be about soldiers form all of the branches of the military that had received the medal in the war they had served. What the Medal Of Honor is when a soldier has done something brave even risking their life above the call of duty. The most received metal of Metal of Honor was during the Civil War(Apr 12, 1861 – May 9, 1865) and the medals received was 1522. The lowest of metals received was during Haiti (1919-1920), Nicaraguan Campaign (1912 – 1933), and Somalia (start date 1991), they all received 2 Medal Of Honors. The branch that received the most medals is the U.S Army with 2404, the U.S Navy with 764, the U.S Marine Corps with 297, the U.S Air Force with 17, and the U.S Coast Guard with only 1.
